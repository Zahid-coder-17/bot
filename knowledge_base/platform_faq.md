# Platform FAQ

## Getting Started

### How do I create an account?
1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Verify your email
5. Complete your profile

### How do I enroll in a course?
1. Browse or search for courses
2. Click on the course you're interested in
3. Review course details and requirements
4. Click "Enroll Now"
5. Complete payment (if applicable)

### Is there a free trial?
Yes, most courses offer a 7-day free trial. During the trial, you can access all course materials but cannot earn a certificate.

## Navigation

### Where do I find my enrolled courses?
Go to Dashboard > My Courses. All your enrolled courses appear here with progress indicators.

### How do I access course materials?
1. Go to My Courses
2. Click on the course
3. Select the module
4. Choose the lesson

### How do I find my certificates?
Go to Profile > Certificates. All earned certificates are available for download here.

### How do I update my profile?
Go to Settings > Profile. You can update:
- Display name
- Profile picture
- Bio
- Notification preferences

## Technical Issues

### Videos won't play. What should I do?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Clear browser cache
4. Disable ad blockers
5. Contact support if issue persists

### Can I download course materials for offline use?
Mobile app users can download videos and readings for offline access. Desktop users can access PDF materials for download where available.

### Which browsers are supported?
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge
- Opera

### What are the system requirements?
- Modern web browser
- Stable internet connection (5+ Mbps recommended)
- JavaScript enabled
- Cookies enabled

## Payments and Subscriptions

### What payment methods are accepted?
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AmEx)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (select regions)
- Education institution payment

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings > Subscription > Cancel. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

### Can I get a refund?
Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase if no certificate has been earned. Contact support for refund requests.

## Support

### How do I contact support?
- Email: support@platform.com
- Live chat: Available on all pages
- Help center: help.platform.com
- Phone: Available for premium members

### What are support hours?
- Live chat: 24/7
- Email: Response within 24 hours
- Phone: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST

### How do I report a bug?
1. Click the feedback button
2. Select "Report a bug"
3. Describe the issue
4. Include screenshots if possible
5. Submit

## Community

### How do I participate in discussions?
Each course has a discussion forum. Navigate to course page > Discussions to post questions or participate.

### Can I connect with other learners?
Yes! You can:
- Participate in course forums
- Join study groups
- Attend virtual office hours
- Network through community events

### Are there live sessions?
Some courses offer:
- Weekly live Q&A with instructors
- Study group sessions
- Career webinars
- Guest speaker events

## Accessibility

### What accessibility features are available?
- Screen reader compatible
- Closed captions on videos
- Adjustable playback speed
- High contrast mode
- Keyboard navigation

### How do I request accommodations?
Contact accessibility@platform.com with:
- Your accommodation needs
- Relevant documentation
- Courses requiring accommodation
